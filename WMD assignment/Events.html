Paris Fashion Week

Paris

Where?Throughout Paris

When? Men and haute couture January and June/July; women March and September/October

New York hosted the world's first "fashion week" in 1943. What exactly is the concept here? To provide fashion customers in New York with alternatives to the French gowns they were missing out on during the war. With devotees arriving en force to its yearly fashion weeks, Paris can still claim to be the world's style capital. Visit the Grand Palais or the Espace Eiffel for a chance to catch celebrities on the runway. Wear your best vintage around the Tuileries and you might be stopped by a street-style photographer for a snapshot.

Fête de la Gastronomie

Paris

Where? Restaurants, markets and ateliers throughout Paris

When ? March

The food of France is excellent. Excellent. In fact, Unesco designated it as part of humanity's "intangible cultural heritage" in 2010. Since then, hundreds of banquets, tastings, workshops, and special market days have taken place across Paris to celebrate all things French culinary (and the rest of the country). The full calendar of events can be found on the website.

Roland Garros

Paris

Where?Stade Roland Garros

When? May/June

Roland Garros is one of the four grand slam tennis championships in the world, and it is usually in high demand. The Philippe Chatrier court hosts the most high-profile matches, while the Suzanne Lenglen and Simonne Mathieu courts also host some dramatic battles with a superior view. Normally, tickets go on sale in mid-March.

Bastille Day

Paris

Where? Locations throughout Paris

When? July

You all know this one. On July 14 France marks the storming of the Bastille prison – a turning point in the French Revolution. In the morning crowds gather to watch a military parade along the Champs-Élysées. Come evening, the Champ de Mars throngs with up to a million spectators as fireworks are set off from the base of the Eiffel Tower. Our favourite vantage point? The top of the Parc de Belleville. Get down early and bring a picnic chair.
